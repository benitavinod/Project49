Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among the Chameleons.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use gto wrap around tree branches.

Interesting Facts about Pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are renowned for their oustanding navigational abilities.
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing ablities.

Interesting Facts about Camel

  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.
  • Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.

Interesting Facts about Teddy-Bears

  • A teddy bear is a stuffed toy in the form of a bear.
  • The first toy stuffed bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff.
  • Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902.

Interesting Facts about Panda

  • Pandas have great camouflage for their environment.
  • They spend a lot of their day eating.
  • Their eyes are different to normal bears.

Interesting Facts about Flying Cicada

  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
  • The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus.
  • Their short adult life is not unusual for bugs.
